MAGGI




international brand of seasonings, instant soups, and noodles

Maggi is an international brand, now owned by Nestlé, that primarily sells instant noodles, soups, and seasonings, originating in Switzerland in the late 19th century. Famous for its quick-to-prepare instant noodle products, such as the "2-Minute Masala Noodles"


Ingredients:


  • 1-2 tablespoons of cooking oil or butter
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • Mixed vegetables like carrots, capsicum, or peas

Steps:

  1. Boil water: In a pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil
  2. Add noodles: Once boiling, add the packet of Maggi noodles and cook for 2-3 minutes until they are soft but still have some texture
  3. Add the flavor sachet: Stir in the included masala (flavor sachet).
  4. Serve: Your basic Maggi is ready to be served hot.
